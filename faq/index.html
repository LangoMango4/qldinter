Frequently Asked Questions

I cannot see any other channels.

Verify your access at: read-me-verification. If you don't do this, you will be unable to see other channels.

Can I join the Police, Ambulance, or Fire services?

Yes, you are welcome to join any of the emergency services here. However, you must join the department's Discord and complete the necessary training.

I'm unable to join the game!

You might be in a blacklisted group, have no active SSU, or there could be ongoing training sessions.

When are group requests and roles checked?

Group requests and roles are checked randomly throughout the day. Keep in mind, they may not be reviewed before an SSU if we are busy.

Can I get a firearm?

You will not receive a firearm by begging a Server Moderator; that approach will not work. You may obtain one if you are involved in a legitimate roleplay situation, but this does not include random deathmatching (RDM) against other players.

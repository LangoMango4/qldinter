Banned Users

Transparency is important to us. Below you'll find a list of all currently banned users from Queensland Interactive. Public details are limited to protect privacy; administrators retain full ban context behind the scenes.

Important: Any bans regarding breaking Terms Of Service (TOS) or Community guidelines are final and not subject to appeal. We fact check all these bans by our System, and if you believe you were banned falsely, please contact us through our Discord.

Fair & Transparent

All bans are issued fairly and documented publicly for community transparency.

Public Transparency

We publish active bans to keep the community informed, while preserving the privacy of moderation details.

Appeal Process

Believe your ban was unfair? You have the right to appeal through our Discord.

Need to Submit an Appeal?

If you believe you were banned unfairly or wish to appeal your ban, please follow our official appeal process below. All appeals are reviewed by senior staff members within 3-7 days or depending on the complexity of the case.

To submit an appeal, please visit the Google Forms Link here and fill out the required information. Be sure to provide as much detail as possible to help us understand your case.

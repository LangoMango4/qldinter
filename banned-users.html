If you believe you were banned unfairly or wish to appeal your ban, please follow our official appeal process. All appeals are reviewed by senior staff members.

To submit an appeal: Join our Discord Server and create a ticket in the support channel. Provide your username, ban reason, and explanation for why you believe the ban should be lifted.

Please note: Appeals for serious violations (hacking, harassment, etc.) are rarely approved. Be respectful and honest in your appeal.

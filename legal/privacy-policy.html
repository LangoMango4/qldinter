Privacy Policy

How Queensland Interactive collects, uses, and protects your personal data while you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us provide, protect, and improve our services. This includes:

  • Account and profile details when you register or join our community.
  • Cookies and usage data to analyze how the website and services are used.
  • Contact details you provide when sending support or feedback messages.

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used to:

  • Deliver and improve our website and Roblox community tools.
  • Protect against abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important updates.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for authentication, analytics, and performance optimization. You can accept or decline cookies through the consent banner shown on first visit.

4. Data Security

We maintain reasonable safeguards to protect your personal information, but no online system is completely secure. If a security issue occurs, we will notify affected users in accordance with applicable law.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at support@queenslandinteractive-rblx.com.

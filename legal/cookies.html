Cookies Policy

Learn what cookies we use, why they matter, and how you can manage cookie settings.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit so it can work more efficiently.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Enable core site functions such as preferences and navigation.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.
  • Performance cookies: Support load balancing and faster page delivery.

3. Your Choices

You can accept or decline cookies using the cookie consent banner that appears on first visit. Declining cookies may limit some interactive features.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services for analytics and security. Those services may set cookies in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Contact

If you have questions about cookie use, email support@queenslandinteractive-rblx.com.

Captcha Policy

Information about our captcha protections, why they are used, and how they help keep the site safe.

1. Why We Use Captcha

Captcha is used to verify that actions are being performed by real people, not automated scripts. This helps protect our site from abuse and spam.

2. What Captcha Protects

Captcha safeguards form submissions, account actions, and any interactive process that could be targeted by bots.

3. Captcha Provider

We may use third-party captcha services such as Cloudflare Turnstile or similar providers. These providers may collect minimal usage data to verify responses.

4. User Impact

Captcha is designed to be easy for legitimate users while stopping abuse. If you experience repeated captcha challenges, please contact support.

5. Contact

If you have questions about captcha or accessibility concerns, email support@queenslandinteractive-rblx.com.
